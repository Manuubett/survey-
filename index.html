📋 Informed Consent

Embu County Political & Service Delivery Survey · ECS-2025 · Please read carefully

1. Purpose of This Research

You are invited to participate in a research study examining political participation, governance, service delivery, and development priorities in Embu County. Findings will inform policy discussions and community development planning.

2. What Participation Involves

Your participation involves completing a questionnaire covering:

  • Your demographic background (age, gender, location, education, employment)
  • Political participation and civic engagement
  • Opinions on local government transparency and accountability
  • Assessment of public service delivery (health, education, agriculture, infrastructure)
  • Economic challenges and development priorities in your ward
  • Sentiment and preferences regarding county leadership
3. Duration

Completing this questionnaire takes approximately 20-35 minutes.

4. Voluntary Participation

Participation is entirely voluntary. You may decline any question and may withdraw at any time without negative consequences.

5. Confidentiality & Data Protection
  • All information is kept strictly confidential.
  • Responses will be anonymised and cannot be traced to you individually.
  • Data is stored securely, accessible only to authorised research personnel.
  • Aggregated findings may be published; no individual data will be disclosed.
  • Contact information (if provided) is stored separately from survey responses.
  • Your data will be handled per applicable data protection laws.
  • GPS coordinates are collected only to verify geographic coverage and prevent duplicate submissions - not to identify individuals.
6. Risks & Benefits

There are no known risks. Your contribution ensures community voices are represented in Embu County governance and policy-making.
