📋 Informed Consent Agreement

Embu County Political & Service Delivery Survey · ECS-2025 · Please read carefully before proceeding

1. Purpose of This Research

You are being invited to participate in a research study examining political participation, governance, service delivery, and development priorities in Embu County. The findings will be used to inform policy discussions and community development planning.

2. What Participation Involves

Your participation involves completing a questionnaire covering:

  • Your demographic background (age, gender, location, education, employment)
  • Political participation and civic engagement
  • Opinions on local government transparency and accountability
  • Assessment of public service delivery (health, education, agriculture, infrastructure)
  • Economic challenges and development priorities in your ward
  • Sentiment and preferences regarding county leadership

3. Duration

Completing this questionnaire is expected to take approximately 20–35 minutes.

4. Voluntary Participation

Your participation is entirely voluntary. You may decline to answer any question and may withdraw from the study at any time without any negative consequences.

5. Confidentiality & Data Protection

  • All information you provide will be kept strictly confidential.
  • Your responses will be anonymised and cannot be traced back to you as an individual.
  • Data will be stored securely and accessible only to authorised research personnel.
  • Aggregated findings may be published in reports, but no individual's data will be disclosed.
  • Contact information provided at the end (if any) will be stored separately from survey responses.
  • Your personal data will be handled in accordance with applicable data protection laws.
  • Location data (GPS coordinates) is collected solely to verify geographic coverage and prevent duplicate submissions. It is not used to identify individuals.

6. Risks & Benefits

There are no known risks associated with participating in this study. Your contribution will help ensure community voices are represented in governance and policy-making in Embu County.
